BIOGRAPHY

Hi I'm Kun-Yao Lin, currently pursuing a Master's degree in Electronics at National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, focusing on parallelization in programming. During my university years, I focused primarily on multimedia chip design, implementing Winograd's Convolution algorithm using Verilog. Additionally, I utilized Python for machine learning purposes, particularly in data analysis. As part of an internship, I joined Ruoshui, where I expanded my skills in both front-end and back-end development, and gained insights into web system architecture. This experience deepened my understanding of the industry and underscored the importance of practical experience.

Following the internship, I transitioned to a research assistant role at National Chengchi University. There, our team replicated experiments from an academic paper and sought to improve the relevant framework to enhance evaluation metrics. Looking ahead, I plan to continue learning, exploring knowledge across different domains, and applying these skills and experiences to address future challenges faced by companies.
